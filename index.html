About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense action survival game where you must fend off endless waves of monsters. Collect experience, upgrade your character, and survive as long as possible in this challenging and addictive experience.

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Weapons fire automatically at nearby enemies. Collect experience gems to level up and choose new weapons or upgrades. The longer you survive, the more difficult the game becomes!

Game Features

Multiple Characters

Choose from various unique characters, each with their own starting weapons and special abilities.

Weapon Upgrades

Discover and upgrade different weapons to create the ultimate monster-slaying arsenal.

Endless Challenge

Face increasingly difficult waves of monsters and see how long you can survive.

Tips for Survival

Keep moving to avoid being surrounded. Prioritize your upgrades based on your playstyle. Don't forget to collect health pickups when they appear. The early game is crucial - make good upgrade choices to set yourself up for success!
